The Island

Hinnavaru

Hinnavaru pairs a lively Maldivian community with the classic postcard ingredients — white sand, a calm lagoon and reefs all around.

The island's dedicated visitors' beach is made for slow days: swim, snorkel, read under the palms, repeat. When you want more, Lhaviyani's sandbanks, reefs and dive sites are a short boat ride away.
